# 🪞 MirrorMood **Real-time facial expression-based mood tracking for Android.** MirrorMood uses your device's front camera and Google ML Kit's face detection to analyze facial expressions in real time, classify your mood, and provide personalized wellness recommendations — all processed entirely on-device for complete privacy. --- ## ✨ Features ### 🎭 Real-Time Mood Detection - Continuous mood monitoring via the front-facing camera as a foreground service - Multi-signal classification using **smile probability**, **eye openness**, **head rotation** (pitch, yaw, roll), **wink detection**, and **rapid blink detection** - Six mood categories: **Happy**, **Stressed**, **Tired**, **Focused**, **Bored**, and **Neutral** - Confidence scoring for each mood classification ### 📊 Insights & Analytics - **Daily breakdown** with per-mood percentages - **Dominant mood** detection with emoji visualization - **Peak time analysis** — see when you're happiest or most stressed - **Monthly bar chart** with 4-week stacked mood distribution (MPAndroidChart) - **Mood correlations** to discover patterns in your emotional data ### 📅 Timeline & History - Chronological timeline view of all mood entries - Full mood history with detailed logs - Streak tracking (e.g., "3-day Happy streak! 🔥") ### 🧘 Wellness Recommendations - **40+ curated wellness tips** organized by mood category - Categories include Breathing exercises, Activities, Mindset shifts, and Self-Care - Contextual wellness card on the home screen that adapts to your current mood - Dedicated recommendations screen with shuffled tips ### 📓 Mood Journal - Personal journaling to reflect on your mood throughout the day - Pairs journal entries with detected mood data ### ⚙️ Settings & Customization - **Theme selection**: Light, Dark, or System Default - **Quiet hours**: Set start/end times to pause notifications - **Daily mood goal**: Target a specific mood at a desired percentage - **App lock**: Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) via AndroidX Biometric ### 🔒 Privacy First - **100% on-device processing** — no images or mood data leave your phone - Dedicated privacy screen explaining data handling - Optional app lock for sensitive mood data - Local Room database storage
