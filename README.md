# 🪞 MirrorMood

**Real-time facial expression-based mood tracking for Android — with Wear OS companion.**

MirrorMood uses your device's front camera and Google ML Kit's face detection to analyze facial expressions in real time, classify your mood, and provide personalized wellness recommendations — all processed entirely on-device for complete privacy. A companion Wear OS app lets you quick-log your mood from your wrist.

> **Note:** For a fully robust feature write-up and component breakdown, please check out [DOCUMENTATION.md](DOCUMENTATION.md).


---

## ✨ Features

### 🎭 Real-Time Mood Detection
- Continuous mood monitoring via the front-facing camera as a foreground service
- **Dual-engine classification**: heuristic multi-signal scoring **plus** an on-device TFLite neural network (FER2013-based) for higher accuracy
- Multi-signal analysis using **smile probability**, **eye openness**, **head rotation** (pitch, yaw, roll), **wink detection**, and **rapid blink detection**
- Six mood categories: **Happy**, **Stressed**, **Tired**, **Focused**, **Bored**, and **Neutral**
- Temporal smoothing with a 5-frame sliding window and 3-frame consensus threshold
- Confidence scoring for each classification
- **Personalized calibration** — an initial face-scanning session captures your unique baseline metrics for improved accuracy

### 📊 Insights & Analytics
- **Today / This Week** tab-based view for switching time ranges
- **Dominant mood** detection with emoji and percentage
- **Per-mood breakdown** with colored pills showing each mood's share
- **Peak time analysis** — discover when you're happiest or most stressed
- **Segmented Mood Bar**: At-a-glance visualization of daily mood distribution with percentages
- **Full-width 7-Day Trend**: Compare your historical mood flow visually with dynamic day labels
- **Mood correlations** — time-of-day & day-of-week pattern analysis
- **Health Connect integration** — correlate mood with sleep quality and step count data
- Dark mode-compatible charts using the Ethereal Archive color system

### 📅 Timeline & History
- Chronological timeline of **all-time** mood entries, grouped by day
- "Today" / "Yesterday" / full-date smart labels
- Calendar-based history view with monthly mood distribution
- Streak tracking (e.g., "3-day Happy streak! 🔥")

### 🧘 Wellness Ecosystem
- **40+ curated wellness tips** organized by mood category
- **5 filter categories**: All, Breathing, Activity, Mindset, and Self-Care
- **Gradient hero-style wellness card** on the home screen that adapts to your current mood
- Dedicated recommendations screen with **category-colored accent bars**, duration chips, and emoji orbs
- **Guided wellness sessions** with three modes:
  - 🫧 **4-7-8 Breathing** — animated inhale / hold / exhale ring with haptic breathing guide
  - 🧘 **Body Scan** — progressive 6-step relaxation sequence
  - ✨ **Gratitude Reflection** — 5 thoughtful prompts with a timed journal flow
- Session completion tracking feeds into the gamification system
- Reduced-motion support for accessibility

### 📓 Mood Journal
- Personal journaling with mood tagging
- **🎙️ Voice journaling** — dictate entries using on-device speech recognition (prefers offline model for privacy)
- **Smart prompts** — AI-style contextual journal prompts generated by the PromptEngine based on:
  - Current mood and streak patterns
  - Time of day (morning / afternoon / evening)
  - Recurring trigger keywords
  - Mood transitions ("You shifted from Stressed to Happy. What changed?")
- **Search** entries by note content or mood keyword
- **Mood filter chips** — filter archive by Happy, Focused, Neutral, Stressed, Tired, or Bored
- Full archive view showing all entries (not just today)
- **Long-press to delete** entries with confirmation dialog
- Editable notes with inline save

### 🏆 Gamification & Achievements
- **15 unlockable achievements** across four categories:
  - **Volume** — First Check-In, Getting Started (10), Half Century (50), Century (100)
  - **Streak** — Three-peat (3 days), Week Warrior (7 days), Monthly Master (30 days)
  - **Exploration** — Emotional Range (4 moods/day), Wellness Explorer (all 6 categories), Storyteller (10 journal entries)
  - **Special** — Night Owl, Early Bird, Zen Master (10 wellness sessions), Health Nut (link Health Connect), Voice Memo (first voice entry)
- **Milestone Engine** — tracks real-time progress with percentage bars
- Achievement grid with animated entrance, progress indicators, and unlock celebrations
- Persisted in Room database via dedicated `AchievementEntity` and `AchievementRepository`

### 🔔 Smart Notifications
- **Morning reminder** — adapts to your actual habits via the **HabitAnalyzer** (learns optimal notification timing from your check-in history)
- **Evening summary** with day's mood recap, **yesterday comparison** (↑/↓ delta), and wellness tip
- **Weekly digest** (Sunday) with dominant mood, **week-over-week trend** comparison, tracking days, and streak info
- **Anomaly alerts** — notified when 75%+ of recent readings show stress/fatigue (via `AnomalyWorker`)
- **Quiet hours** support — disable notifications during set time windows
- **Weekend-aware scheduling** — detects different engagement patterns on weekends
- Powered by WorkManager with Hilt-injected workers

### ⌚ Wear OS Companion
- **1-tap Quick Log** — log your mood directly from your wrist with 6 emotion buttons
- Syncs mood entries to the phone app via the **Wearable Data Layer API** in real time
- Phone-side `WearDataReceiverService` automatically writes entries to the shared Room database
- Entries are tagged as "Quick logged from Wear OS" in the journal

### 🏠 Home Screen Widget
- At-a-glance mood widget showing your current mood emoji and label
- **Auto-updates** immediately when a new mood is detected
- Tap to open the app directly

### 💚 Health Connect Integration
- Read **sleep duration**, **sleep quality scores**, and **step count** from Health Connect
- **Sleep quality scoring** (0–100) based on duration consistency, total hours, and regularity
- Dashboard health snapshot card showing today's steps, sleep hours, and quality recommendation
- Correlate health metrics with mood patterns for deeper insights
- Graceful fallback when Health Connect is unavailable

### 📋 Onboarding & Calibration
- **Welcome onboarding** screen with privacy-first messaging and quick-start flow
- **Face calibration** — guided camera session that captures your neutral facial baseline (smile, eye openness, head angles) for personalized mood detection
- Auto-skips on subsequent launches once completed
- Smooth flow: Onboarding → Calibration → Lock Screen → Dashboard

### ⚙️ Settings & Customization
- **Theme selection**: Light, Dark, or System Default
- **Quiet hours**: Set start/end times to pause monitoring
- **Pause monitoring**: One-tap 24-hour pause with auto-resume
- **Daily mood goal**: Target a specific mood percentage with a slider
- **App lock**: Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) via AndroidX Biometric **+ PIN fallback** with salted SHA-256 hashing
- **Reduced motion**: Accessibility option to disable animations
- **Export data**: Download all mood entries as a CSV file via the share sheet
- **Automated JSON backups**: Scheduled `BackupWorker` creates rolling JSON backups (keeps last 5)
- **Delete all data**: Erase all entries with confirmation

### 🔒 Privacy First
- **100% on-device processing** — no images or mood data leave your phone
- TFLite model runs locally; voice recognition prefers offline mode
- Dedicated privacy screen explaining data handling
- [Full Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md)
- Optional biometric + PIN app lock for sensitive mood data
- Local Room database storage only

## 🎨 Design System — Ethereal Archive

MirrorMood uses a custom "Ethereal Archive" design system instead of Material You's wallpaper-based dynamic colors:

- **Surface hierarchy**: 6-level tonal system (`mm_surface` → `mm_surface_container_highest`)
- **No-Line philosophy**: Cards use tonal elevation instead of borders
- **Custom typography**: Manrope font family
- **Mood palette**: Each mood has distinct light and dark variants
- **Full dark mode** support via `values-night` resource qualifiers
- **Slide transitions**: Consistent activity slide animations across all screens

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Android Studio Ladybug (2024.2.1) or later
- JDK 11+
- Android device or emulator with a front-facing camera (API 26+)
- *(Optional)* Wear OS device or emulator for the companion app

### Setup

1. **Clone the repository**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/arjunav2016-bit/MirrorMood.git
   cd MirrorMood
   ```

2. **Open in Android Studio**
   - File → Open → select the `MirrorMood` directory

3. **Sync Gradle**
   - Android Studio will auto-sync; if not, click **Sync Now**

4. **Run the app**
   - Select a physical device or emulator with a camera
   - Click ▶️ **Run** on the `:app` module

5. **Run the Wear OS companion** *(optional)*
   - Select a Wear OS device or emulator
   - Click ▶️ **Run** on the `:wear` module

### Permissions
The app requests the following permissions at runtime:
- **Camera** — required for facial expression analysis
- **Notifications** (Android 13+) — for mood reminders and summaries
- **Microphone** — for voice journaling *(optional)*
- **Health Connect** — for sleep and step data correlation *(optional)*

## 📸 How It Works

1. **Complete onboarding** — first launch walks you through privacy info and face calibration
2. **Tap "Start Monitoring"** on the home screen
3. The app launches a **foreground service** with CameraX accessing the front camera
4. **ML Kit Face Detection** processes each frame to extract:
   - Smile probability
   - Left/right eye openness
   - Head Euler angles (pitch, yaw, roll)
   - Blink and wink events
5. **Dual classification**:
   - **MoodClassifier** scores each mood using weighted heuristic signals + contextual boosts
   - **TFLiteMoodClassifier** runs a neural network on the face crop for expression recognition
6. **FaceAnalyzer** uses a 5-frame sliding window — mood is only saved when 3+ frames agree
7. Results are saved to the local **Room database** with timestamps
8. The **home screen widget** auto-refreshes after every new entry
9. The UI updates in real-time with mood emoji, label, color, and wellness tips
10. **Achievements** are automatically checked and unlocked after each new entry

### Smart Monitoring
- When the screen turns off, the service switches to **battery-saving mode**: brief 5-second captures every 60 seconds
- **Quiet hours** and **pause toggle** suspend the camera entirely
- 24-hour pause auto-resumes without user intervention

### Wear OS Quick Log
- Open the MirrorMood watch app → tap a mood button → entry syncs to phone via Data Layer API
- No camera needed — manual 1-tap logging from your wrist
